About Us

We Help People In Need Around The World

At FGCK Northern Bypass L.C.A, we believe in being the hands and feet of Jesus. Our mission is to support and uplift the needy around the world through acts of kindness, compassion, and faith-driven service. Together, we bring hope where it’s needed most.

Rev. Johnstone Gathogo, L.C.A Overseer

Around the world, there are people in need — and we believe God has called us to make a difference. Through giving, prayer, and compassion, we reach out to those in need, bringing hope, food, and faith to every community we touch. Together, we can change lives and reflect God’s love in action.
